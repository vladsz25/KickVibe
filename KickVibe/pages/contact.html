Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days, and overnight shipping arrives the next business day.

Do you offer returns? +

Yes! We offer 30-day returns on unworn, unused items with original packaging. For more information, visit our returns policy page.

Are your sneakers authentic? +

Absolutely! All our sneakers are 100% authentic and sourced directly from authorized retailers and brand partners.

Do you ship internationally? +

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location. Contact us for more details.

How can I track my order? +

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. You can also track your order in your account dashboard.

What if my item arrives damaged? +

Contact our support team with photos of the damage within 48 hours of delivery. We'll arrange a replacement or refund immediately.
